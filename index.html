// how it works

Selection to dispense in 30 seconds.

The Identivend AV3000 age verification system makes age-restricted vending faster and safer than a human transaction. Four steps, biometric AI throughout.

01

Select

Customer browses the touchscreen — full product images, descriptions, even promo videos. Selects what they want.

02

Face scan

3D biometric scan validates a real human at the machine. No app. No phone. No account creation required.

03

ID check

Customer presents ID to the scanner. Real-time AI authenticates holograms + UV security features + matches photo to face.

04

Dispense

Verified and of age: payment unlocks. Tap, chip, Apple Pay, Google Wallet — all accepted. Product delivered.
