// how it works

Premium tobacco. Verified buyer. One minute.

The Identivend AV3000 age verification system makes age-restricted vending safer and faster than a human transaction. Four steps from selection to dispense.

01

Select a product

Customer browses the 22″ touchscreen — full product images, descriptions, even promo videos. Selects what they want.

02

Face scan

3D biometric scan validates a real human in front of the machine. No app. No account. No identity stored.

ID authentication

Customer presents their ID to the scanner. Real-time authentication matches the ID to the 3D face scan.

Payment unlocked

If verified and over-21: payment unlocked. Tap, chip, Apple Pay, Google Wallet — all accepted. Product dispensed.
